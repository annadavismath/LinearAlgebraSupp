\begin{exercise}
Find the inverse of the following matrix.
$$A=\begin{bmatrix}1 & -1 & 2\\-5 & 7 & -11\\-2 & 3 &-5\end{bmatrix}$$
$$A^{-1}=\begin{bmatrix}\answer{2} & \answer{-1} & \answer{3}\\\answer{3} & \answer{1} & \answer{-1}\\\answer{1} & \answer{1} &\answer{-2}\end{bmatrix}$$
 
\end{exercise}
