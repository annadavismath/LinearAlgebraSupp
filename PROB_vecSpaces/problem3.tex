\begin{exercise}
Find the coordinates of $\vec{v}=ax^2+bx+c$ of $\mathbb{P}^2$ with respect to the ordered basis $\mathcal{B}=\left\{x^2, x+1, x+2\right\}$.
$$[\vec{v}]_{\mathcal{B}}=\begin{bmatrix}\answer{a}\\\answer{2b-c}\\\answer{c-b}\end{bmatrix}$$

 \end{exercise}
