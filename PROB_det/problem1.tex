\begin{exercise}
Find the determinant of $A$ if 
$$A=\begin{bmatrix}1 & 3 & 4 & 1\\k & 0 & 0 & 2\\0 & 1 & -2 & 1\\1 & -1 & 0 & 1\end{bmatrix}$$
where $k$ is a constant.

$$\mbox{det}(A)=\answer{16k-24}$$

For what value of $k$ is $A$ singular?

$$k=\answer{1.5}$$
