\begin{exercise}
Find the measure of the acute angle (in degrees) of intersection of lines $L_1$ and $L_2$ given by parametric equations

$$L_1:\quad x=t,\,y=t,\,z=0$$
$$L_2:\quad x=2t,\,y=t,\,z=t$$

$$\answer{30}\, \mbox{degrees}$$
 
\end{exercise}
